About Me

Note that this site contains spoilers for Eragon, by Christopher Paolini.

Biography

Growing up in the small town of Carvahall, I was raised only by my grandfather, and without an idea as to the identities of my mother or father. As we were a small household (between myself, my brother, and my grandfather), we scraped by through hunting in the wilderness. One day, while out on a hunt, I came across a dragon's egg, which I brought home and hatched! Over time, we were able to establish a mental connection that allowed sharing thoughts, feelings, and even sights.

Unfortunately, the extremely oppressive government got wind of the egg's location and came after it via the Ra'zac, a vile species that imitates humans to get close to them and eat their flesh & bone marrow. They killed my grandfather and burned down my house, forcing me to chase after them in an effort to get vengence and train to become a true dragon rider and free all the people of the aforementioned oppressive government.
