My Tutelage under Brom

My favorite class under Brom was learning magic in an informal setting, accomplishing simple tasks, such as lifting stones, and making explosions

My favorite memory with Brom was when he would tell me stories about the times before when Galbatorix had taken over the world, as we knew it.

My Tutelage under Oromis

My favorite class under Oromis was watching nature and learning to listen to all the thoughts of the insects and animals around me to gain a greater appreciation for life and be able to surveil everyone's intentions around me.

My favorite memory with Oromis was when he helped me understand why we faught against the empire when doing so would bring about a significant amount of suffering (it was more than the suffering brought about by the existing government, and due to the dictator's infinite lifespan, it couldn't end without someone rising up against him).
